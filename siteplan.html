Site Plan: Pax & Pa's Handmade Pens

Prepared by Paxton Porter

Pax & Pa's

Handmade Pens

A small business specializing in handcrafted wooden pens made from locally sourced materials. Each pen is unique, showcasing the natural beauty of the wood combined with quality craftsmanship.

Optional domain availability: pax&pas.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online portfolio and store for Pax & Pa's Handmade Pens. It will showcase the different pen styles available, provide information about the crafting process, and allow customers to make purchases directly through the site.

Pens

Color Scheme

These colors bring a warm and outdoorsy vibe, enhancing the custom and locally sourced materials

Primary Color – Forest Green (#2b5f2f) Used for: headings, header background, buttons

Secondary Color – Warm Tan (#f4e7c3) Used for: main background, section backgrounds

Accent Color – Rustic Red (#c94f3a) Used for: highlights, icons, emphasis elements

Typography

Heading Font: Merriweather (Serif)

Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to give a classic outdoor travel feel.

Body Font: Montserrat (Sans-Serif)

Clean and modern for paragraphs, buttons, labels, and navigation.
